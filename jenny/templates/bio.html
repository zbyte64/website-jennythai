Biography

Jenny Thai is an award-winning, service oriented photography studio specializing in interiors and architecture. We have provided high quality photography both in studio and on location since 1997.

Our services begin with meeting with our clients in person to capture their vision and other pre-production ideas, followed by the actual shoot, and concluding with post-production digital processing, prints and album ordering.

Jenny began photographing as an undergraduate at the renowned Institute of Artistic Studies while working on a B.A. in Photojournalism. In a recent interview with Professional Photographer Magazine Elle recalled, "Ever since I can remember I have been captivated by the idea of storytelling with images. I have a genuine appreciation for all of my clients and love 'going for broke' when it comes to creating images that will exceed their expectations."
